The Challenge

For our project we had to make a concept in which we make the journey of the user when they’re on a trip easier. The problem I tried to tackle was scheduling and budgeting your trip with friends.
From concepting and looking at the problem through own experience, I came up with: Paper.

The Solution

Paper is a multidevice application in which you can plan your trip by discussing it with friends, which you invite through the system. You yourself can make a trip, or let somebody else make it and get invited The most important aspects of this concept are: - Have an easier way to communicatie your trip with friends - Budget it and plan your activities accordingly

Extra for the project

To get the concept together i made use of a design system and made a logo to add to the concept. It was important to make this concept visual and understandable to the user through good visuals.
