General Information

Year: 2019
Instructing party: University of Applied Sciences
Category: Webdesign
Class: Vormgeving 2

The Challenge

For this task, the challenge was to create a redesign for the journalist website called HvanA, on multiple platforms. In this redesign, you had to keep the branding and corporate identity in tact.
To make this redesign, we were assigned content to use for this site, and show it in the way we wanted.

The Solution
Check the prototype out

It was important for this project to keep the colorful style of the site the same. Because the colors are very different and not totally complimentary, I had to find a way to make sure everything came back, but the user wouldn’t be confused or irritated by the different colors used at the same time. On the page it was important to keep margins for every section the same, this was a challenge because this was the first time i focused on keeping the margins and white space at it’s minimum.

The Iterations

Before i got to the design I made, I made different iterations of how the articles should be shown and played with the best mix of colours. Here you can see different designs, which weren’t added because of bad margins or the fact it wouldn’t be a good fit with the brand identity.
