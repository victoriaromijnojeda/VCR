General Information

Year: 2019
Instructing party: University of Applied Sciences
Category: Webdesign
Class: VID

The Challenge

The class was tasked with making a design incorporating important aspects of the library named: Biblion. In this we had to take the brand identity and got to play with it freely.
In this design I tried challenging myself by incorporating bold colours and playful illustrations. The reason for this was to go further and bend the rules the way i wanted to as designer.

The Solution

I challenged myself by making animations to make the interface come to live, i tried incorporating the most important colors of the brand identity and used a white version of their logo.
One of the most difficult things I had to do was make a design that incorporated all the colors of the brand, without it becoming too much for the user. I used the different colours to my advantage by grouping them and then attaching certain options to it.
for example: The wishlist only incorporating yellow colors.

The Iterations

One of the older iterations included one main colour, which was purple at that time. I tried playing with gradients and opacities but got as feedback that it was too much focused on one single color which made the brand identity almost go unnoticed if you left the logo out of sight.
